How Ember works

Ember helps you track and analyze pain episodes. By recording your experiences, you can identify patterns and share detailed information with your healthcare providers.

Track your pain

Use our simple form to record your mood level, overall pain intensity, sleep quality and duration, activity interference due to pain, alertness level, and medications used.

Start tracking your pain

Visualize patterns

See your pain data displayed in easy-to-understand charts and graphs. Identify patterns related to time of day, activities, or medication schedules.
