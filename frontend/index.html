How CAFYP works

CAFYP (Care Assistant For Your Pain) helps you track and analyze pain episodes related to Parkinson's disease. By recording your experiences, you can identify patterns and share detailed information with your healthcare providers.

Track your pain

Use our simple form to record pain episodes when they occur. Note the location, intensity, duration, and factors that might be relevant.

Start tracking your pain

Visualize patterns

See your pain data displayed in easy-to-understand charts and graphs. Identify patterns related to time of day, activities, or medication schedules.
